GRU

Felonius Gru, Sr., also known as just Gru, was born on May 25, 1965. He is the primary character in the Despicable Me franchise and the son of Marlena Gru and the late Robert Gru. He is also the grandson of Madeleine Gru and the late Henry Gru, as well as the late John Gru. He is the spouse of AVL agent Lucy Wilde and a former supervillain. Margaret, Edith, and Agnes are his three adopted daughters. In addition to being the twin brother of Dru Gru, Gru is also the leader of the Minions and buddies with Dr. Nefario.

Minion

The Minions are small, yellow capsule-shaped creatures with round gray goggles, depicted as being roughly one-third to one-half the height of humans. They are known for their loyalty and dedication to their evil boss, Gru, in the Despicable Me franchise. The Minions are also known for their gibberish language and their love for bananas. In the Minions film, an 11-year-old Gru plans to become a supervillain with the help of his Minions, which leads to a showdown with a malevolent team, the Vicious 6. The plot of the film follows three Minions as they search for a new evil boss to rule their species.
